Claudia

Co-Founder

Claudia Saavedra is an immigrant from Chile and a first-generation college student. Despite circumstances, she received 1M dollars in scholarships to fund her education. As an undergraduate student, she attended Rutgers University. In college, she traveled to 20 states- some states 2-3 times all expenses paid. Currently, she is studying her Masters in Public Administration at Rutgers University. When she is not working, she loves hiking and kayaking.
